# MASTER SYLLABUS
## Complete Photography & Mindfulness Program

### Program Overview

**Total Content:** 78+ themed sessions across 5 major components  
**Time Commitment:** 6 months to 2+ years depending on path  
**Level:** Beginner to Advanced (all levels welcome)  
**Equipment:** Any camera including smartphone  
**Location:** Customized for Portland/Murrayhill, adaptable anywhere

---

## üìö COURSE COMPONENTS

### Core Course
**10-Week Photography & Mindfulness Journey**
- Duration: 10 weeks (Oct 25, 2025 - Jan 2, 2026)
- Time: 20-30 min daily + weekend excursions
- Focus: Seasonal mindful photography practice in Portland
- Includes: Meditations, photo projects, Portland locations, playlists, photographer references

### Supplementary Courses (Choose Your Path)

**Supplement A: The Mindful Photographer (Howarth)**
- 20 concepts for deepening practice
- Can be done alongside or after core course
- Add 1-2 concepts monthly

**Supplement B: Art of Seeing (Patterson)**  
- Technical and perceptual training
- Visual design fundamentals
- Breaking creative barriers
- Reference material for ongoing practice

**Supplement C: SINS Workshop**
- 8 sessions exploring contemporary morality
- Edgy, confrontational themes
- 4 weeks intensive or self-paced
- For photographers wanting provocative work

**Supplement D: I AM Self-Portrait Workshop**
- 15 sessions on identity through self-portraiture
- 5 weeks or self-paced
- Deep personal exploration
- Can be private or shared work

**Supplement E: 26 Activities for Self-Knowledge**
- 26-week comprehensive self-discovery
- 6 modules building on each other
- Therapeutic photography approach
- Ongoing reference material

## üõ§Ô∏è LEARNING PATHWAYS

### PATH 1: BEGINNER'S JOURNEY (6 months)

**Perfect for:** Complete beginners, those new to mindfulness or photography

**Month 1-2.5: Core Foundation**
- Complete 10-Week Core Course (Oct 25 - Jan 2)
- Daily practice in Portland/Murrayhill
- Build foundational skills

**Month 3-4: Deepen Understanding**
- Add 2 Howarth Concepts per month (Clarity, Curiosity, Confidence, Patience)
- Review Patterson's "Barriers to Seeing" section
- Continue daily photography practice

**Month 5-6: Personal Exploration**
- Choose 8 activities from 26 Activities course
- Focus on Module 1 (Foundations) and Module 2 (Body)
- Begin developing authentic voice

**Outcome:** Solid mindful photography foundation, personal daily practice established

---

### PATH 2: INTENSIVE IMMERSION (6 months)

**Perfect for:** Motivated learners, those with time to dedicate, people in transition

**Month 1-2.5: Core Course**
- 10-Week Core Course (Oct 25 - Jan 2)
- Full commitment to daily practice

**Month 3-3.5: Self-Portrait Deep Dive**
- I AM Self-Portrait Workshop (15 sessions)
- 3 sessions per week
- Intensive self-exploration

**Month 4-9: Self-Knowledge Journey**
- 26 Activities for Self-Knowledge
- One activity per week
- Deep therapeutic work

**Ongoing Throughout:**
- Reference Patterson techniques as needed
- Add Howarth concepts monthly
- Consider SINS if drawn to shadow work

**Outcome:** Complete transformation, comprehensive self-knowledge, mature practice

---

### PATH 3: TECHNICAL + MINDFUL (8 months)

**Perfect for:** Photographers wanting to improve craft while developing awareness

**Month 1-2.5: Core Foundation**
- 10-Week Core Course (Oct 25 - Jan 2)
- Establish mindfulness practice

**Month 3-5: Technical Mastery**
- Study Patterson's Art of Seeing thoroughly
- Complete all exercises in thinking
- Practice visual design principles
- One chapter per week

**Month 6-8: Integration**
- 12 Howarth concepts (3 per month)
- Apply technical skills to mindful seeing
- Create portfolio combining both approaches

**Outcome:** Technically strong AND mindfully aware photographer

---

### PATH 4: THERAPEUTIC EXPLORATION (10 months)

**Perfect for:** Those seeking healing, self-understanding, personal growth

**Month 1-2.5: Gentle Beginning**
- 10-Week Core Course (Oct 25 - Jan 2)
- Establish safe daily practice

**Month 3-8: Deep Self-Work**
- 26 Activities for Self-Knowledge (one per week)
- Take breaks as needed
- Journal extensively

**Month 9-10: Integration & Expression**
- I AM Self-Portrait Workshop (15 sessions)
- Synthesize learning
- Create meaningful portfolio

**Optional:** Add SINS workshop if ready for shadow work

**Outcome:** Deep self-knowledge, healing through photography, authentic expression

---

### PATH 5: CREATIVE BREAKTHROUGH (4 months)

**Perfect for:** Experienced photographers feeling stuck, seeking fresh vision

**Month 1-2.5: Core Reset**
- 10-Week Core Course (Oct 25 - Jan 2)
- Return to beginner's mind

**Month 3: Break All Rules**
- SINS Workshop (8 sessions over 4 weeks)
- Challenge assumptions
- Try forbidden subjects

**Month 4: Reconstruct Vision**
- Patterson's "Breaking Rules Systematically"
- Patterson's "Thinking Sideways"
- 6-8 Howarth concepts focusing on playfulness, courage, ambiguity
- Create new body of work

**Outcome:** Creative breakthrough, fresh vision, renewed enthusiasm

---

### PATH 6: COMPREHENSIVE MASTERY (18-24 months)

**Perfect for:** Serious students, those considering teaching, complete transformation seekers

**Phase 1 (Months 1-3): Foundation**
- 10-Week Core Course
- Study Patterson's entire Art of Seeing
- Begin Howarth concepts (1 per month)

**Phase 2 (Months 4-9): Deep Exploration**
- 26 Activities for Self-Knowledge
- Continue Howarth concepts
- Apply Patterson techniques

**Phase 3 (Months 10-11): Shadow & Self**
- SINS Workshop
- I AM Self-Portrait Workshop
- Integrate all learning

**Phase 4 (Months 12-18): Mastery Practice**
- Review and repeat favorite exercises
- Develop teaching materials if interested
- Create comprehensive portfolio
- Establish sustainable practice

**Phase 5 (Months 19-24): Teaching & Sharing**
- Lead workshops using these materials
- Mentor others
- Share your journey
- Continue evolving practice

**Outcome:** Complete mastery, possible teaching career, transformed relationship with seeing
