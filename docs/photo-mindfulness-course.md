## Additional Mindfulness Exercises to Practice Alongside Photography

### Morning Rituals (5-10 minutes)
- **Gratitude Gaze:** Before getting out of bed, look around your room and mentally photograph three things you're grateful for
- **Intention Setting:** Set a visual intention for the day: "Today I will notice..."

### Throughout the Day
- **Three-Breath Reset:** Whenever you feel stressed, take three conscious breaths and notice one beautiful thing
- **Mindful Meals:** Before eating, photograph your food with awareness, noticing colors, arrangement, light
- **Tech Boundary:** One hour before bed, put your phone down and simply observe your environment with naked eyes

### Evening Practices (10-15 minutes)
- **Day Review:** Look through photos you took today‚Äîwhich moment was most present?
- **Gratitude Journaling:** Write three things you saw today that brought you joy
- **Body Scan:** Lie down and scan your body, releasing any visual tension from the day

### Weekly Practices
- **Silent Hour:** One hour per week in complete silence, photographing contemplatively
- **Nature Immersion:** Spend 2 hours in nature with minimal shooting‚Äî90% observing, 10% photographing

## Portland Fall/Winter Photography Practical Guide

**Essential Gear for Portland Rain Season:**
- **Camera protection:** Plastic bag, rain sleeve, or purpose-built rain cover
- **Lens cloth:** Always needed for rain spots on lens
- **Waterproof bag:** Protect gear between shots

- **Microfiber towel:** Dry your gear when you get home

**Working with Portland's Unique Light:**
- **Gray days are your friend:** Soft, diffused light perfect for portraits and details
- **No harsh shadows:** Portland's cloud cover eliminates hard contrast
- **Rain creates reflections:** Shoot puddles, wet pavement, raindrops on windows
