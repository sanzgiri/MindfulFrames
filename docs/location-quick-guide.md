## üì∏ EMERGENCY "I'M STUCK" IDEAS

**Can't decide where to go?**
1. **Flip a coin:** Heads = Murrayhill Park, Tails = Progress Lake
2. **Go where you've never been:** Explore a Murrayhill street you always skip
3. **Opposite direction:** Usually go left? Go right today
4. **Worst weather:** Make that the destination - photograph the rain

1. **Photograph your feet** walking for 10 minutes
2. **One color only:** Find and photograph only blue things (or red, or green)
3. **Ground level:** Photograph from lying down
4. **Sky only:** No ground in frame for entire walk
5. **Shapes:** Only circles, or only lines, or only triangles
