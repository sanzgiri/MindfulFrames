## üì∏ WEATHER-SPECIFIC PHOTO OPPORTUNITIES

### November in Murrayhill:
- **Early month:** Peak fall color, crisp mornings
- **Mid-month:** Leaf fall, bare branches appearing
- **Late month:** First frosts, darker evenings by 5pm
- **Rain frequency:** Increasing, 60-70% of days
- **Best light:** Soft gray, occasional sun breaks
- **Photo ops:** Wet leaves, puddle reflections, mist in parks

### December in Murrayhill:
- **Weather:** Cold, rainy, occasional frost or rare snow
- **Light:** Shortest days (sunset by 4:30pm)
- **Photo ops:** 
  - Holiday lights at Progress Ridge
  - Bare tree silhouettes
  - Winter Solstice darkness (Dec 21)
  - Frost patterns (if cold enough)
  - Fog in early mornings at parks
  - Cozy indoor warmth vs. cold outside contrast
